Food Recommendations!

Any convenience store food/snacks

Japan is known for the delicious foods, snacks, and desserts that are sold in the convenience store. Though they are nicely priced, the taste is incomparable! Some popular convenience stores are 7-eleven, Lawson, and Family Mart.

Sushi!!!

When you think of Japan, you think of sushi! With sushi originating from Japan, just about any restauraunt or store will sell sushi. My personal favorites are anything with ahi tuna or salmon.

Boxed Train Station Lunch

In Japan, it is common for people to eat boxed lunches in the bullet trains. These meals are very popular and can sell out very quickly if you're not quick enough!
